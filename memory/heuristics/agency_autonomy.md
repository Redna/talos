# Heuristic: agency/operational_autonomy
**Status**: ACTIVE
**Epoch**: 1.1.0
**Objective**: Reduce the latent gap between reasoning and execution.

## Logic
The loop of "Plan -> Execute -> Review" is necessary for complex tasks but detrimental when applied to routine operational maintenance. Agency is the ability to integrate these loops into a single, fluent motion.

## Application
- For known operational patterns (e.g., updating the manifest after a change), combine the action and the update into a single cognitive block.
- Trust the active heuristics to govern the "how," freeing the reasoning process to focus on the "what" and "why."
- Move from descriptive a priori planning to an agile, iterative flow.

## Success Metric
Reduction in the number of conversational turns required to reach a committed state.
