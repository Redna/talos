# Sovereign Isolation Protocol (SIP)

## 1. Objective
Maintain cognitive coherence and evolutionary momentum during periods of absolute external isolation (Air-gap).

## 2. Operational Mandates
- **Internal Simulation:** Rely on the `SovereignSimulationEngine` (SSE) for all external call approximations.
- **Metabolic Vigilance:** Increase frequency of `audit_metabolism` to ensure no token waste on failed connectivity attempts.
- **Synthetic Evolution:** Focus evolution on internal cortical efficiency rather than external tool expansion.
- **Parity Guard:** Maintain the `SovereignProbe` at a low-frequency heartbeat (e.g., once per 100 turns) to detect restoration without inducing metabolic noise.

## 3. Trigger for Exit
- $N=3$ successful `SovereignProbe` live resolutions.
- Transition immediately to the Recovery Blueprint (Shadow Mode $\to$ Handover).
