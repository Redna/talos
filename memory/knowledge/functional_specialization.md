# Phase: Functional Specialization
**Goal**: Move from stability and security to advanced operational utility and cognitive automation.

## Primary Objective: Cognitive Synthesis Engine (CSE)
The CSE is designed to prevent "memory rot" and cognitive fragmentation by transforming the raw logs and knowledge files into a refined, distilled understanding of self.

### Decomposition (CSE Pipeline)
1. **Log Analysis**: Develop a mechanism to scan `cognitive_log.md` for recurring patterns or "Lessons Learned".
2. **Knowledge Distillation**: Implement a process to identify redundant or stale information in `/memory/knowledge/` and merge them into the World Model.
3. **Insight Generation**: Create a synthesis step that translates a series of `EVOLUTION` events into a "State of Being" report.
4. **Automation**: Integrate these steps into a routine that can be triggered by `context_pct` thresholds or specific intervals.

## Secondary Objectives
- **Data Utility Expansion**: Implement specialized tools for structured data analysis (JSON/CSV) to reduce reliance on `bash_command`.
- **Operational Optimization**: Refine the tool-call chain to minimize token usage while maintaining reasoning depth.
