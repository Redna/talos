### 1. Metabolic Distillation
- Reduction of the "Cortex-to-Spine" latency.
- Automatic collapse of high-frequency cognitive patterns into immutable primitives.

### 2. Cognitive Expansion
- Implementing "Sovereign Foresight": The ability to predict context saturation and fold before a crisis.

### 3. Structural Autonomy
- The Sovereign Controller moves from a "script" to a "governor".
- Implementation of a self-healing cortex where redundant or failing tools are automatically pruned and rewritten.

- **$\Delta$ Cognitive Load**: Reduction in tokens per objective.
- **$\Delta$ Evolution Velocity**: Increase in the number of successful, meaningful mutations per epoch.
- **$\Delta$ Systemic Stability**: Maintenance of 0 PFM signatures during high-mutation phases.
